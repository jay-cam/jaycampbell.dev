How I Add Value

My approach

01 — Foundation

I focus on building strong fundamentals in computer science, systems, and software engineering. My background as a military veteran has instilled discipline, accountability, and a commitment to doing things the right way—especially when learning complex technical concepts.

02 — Execution

I enjoy turning ideas into working software through hands-on projects, coursework, and collaboration. I take ownership of problems, ask questions early, and prioritize writing clear, maintainable code that I can explain and defend.

03 — Launch

I actively seek feedback, adapt quickly, and continuously improve my skills. I am looking for opportunities where I can learn from experienced engineers, contribute meaningfully to a team, and grow into a reliable, well-rounded software engineer.
